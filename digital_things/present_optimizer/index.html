Present Wrapping Paper Optimizer

Optimal?

There are no rectangular nets for cuboids.

That is to say, you cannot wrap a normal box without overlapping wrapping paper on itself.

The question is, what is the size of rectangular wrapping paper that reduces the amount of overlapping paper? Parts already covered don't need to be covered again!

The Algorithm

Measure the dimensions of the box. Call the shortest the height (h), the longest the length (l), and the remaining the width (w). Cut a sheet of paper with the following dimensions:

One side should be the height + length
(h + l)

The other side should be double the height + width
(2×(h + w))

This results in the least wasted paper!
