Welcome to Windows XP Rainbows X3

This is still a demonstration of what I wanna do with this site! This may become a page for links to friends/interesting sites.

This functions as one would expect:

  • Drag the title bar to move windows
  • Minimize to keep the window here but make it invisible
  • *Maximize to open in a new tab*
  • Close to remove the window entirely
  • Click a title on the taskbar to minimize/restore a window
  • Click the mute button (bottom right) to mute the startup/shutdown sounds
  • Click the start button to open up new windows
  • Turn off the computer to go back home

The way this functions is using the magic of the iframe html element to embed any website into this one! I did a lot of styles to make things look good enough. Everything opened in a window on this site was not made by me. I do not claim responsibility for any fun experiences you may have on other sites seen here :'3
